Privacy Policy


Last Updated: 4 TH DEC 2024

HakiCAR Hospitality Consultancy (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates the website hakicar.com (the “Service”).

This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information when you use our Service. By using the Service, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled securely and transparently.



Information Collection and Use

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that helps us contact or identify you. This information (“Personal Information”) may include, but is not limited to:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Telephone Number
  • Residential or Business Address

We collect this information to provide and improve our Service, communicate effectively, and meet your specific needs.


Log Data

When you visit our Service, we may collect information that your browser sends automatically (“Log Data”). This Log Data may include:

  • Your device’s Internet Protocol (“IP”) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Duration of your visit
  • Other analytical data

We use this data to monitor and enhance the performance of our website, understand user preferences, and improve your overall experience.


Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us track your activity on our Service. These cookies may contain an anonymous unique identifier. We use cookies to:

  • Understand and save user preferences for future visits
  • Compile aggregate data about site traffic and interactions to improve site functionality

You can set your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being used. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our Service.


Service Providers

We may engage third-party companies or individuals to:

  • Facilitate the provision of our Service
  • Perform Service-related functions
  • Analyze usage of our Service

These third parties will only have access to Personal Information necessary to perform these tasks on our behalf. They are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your information for other purposes.


Security

We are committed to safeguarding your Personal Information. While we employ commercially acceptable security practices, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection, but we continually improve our systems to minimize risks.


Links to Other Sites

Our Service may contain links to external websites. These third-party sites are not under our control, and we are not responsible for their content, policies, or practices. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policies of any third-party sites you visit.


Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18 (“Children”). We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with Personal Information, please contact us immediately. Upon verification, we will delete the information from our records.


Compliance With Laws

We may disclose your Personal Information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page and are effective immediately upon posting. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
